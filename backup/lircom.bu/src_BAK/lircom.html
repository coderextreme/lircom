To connect to the chat:
  • Click through security warnings.
  • Type in your nickname.
  • When the "Form a connection" window appears, select the connect button near the bottom of the window.
  • Chat by typing in the black area at the bottom of the colored window.
  • People will only appear on your screen if they type to you, thus you need to type to let people know you are there.
  • Normally, all messages are broadcast. To select someone to talk to, pick their name in the list on the right.
